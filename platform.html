Backend Plan

Owned, polished, and not $199/month.

Use GitHub Pages for the professional front door. Use a self-hosted community platform later when the waitlist and founding beta prove there are real conversations to support.

Recommended stack

Front Door

GitHub Pages hosting this static site at a clean domain or subdomain.

Waitlist

Wix Forms, Brevo, MailerLite, Formspree, or Google Forms for invite requests.

Community

Self-hosted Discourse on community.throupletea.com once beta demand is real.

Why this works

  • You own the brand, domain, waitlist, and member relationship.
  • GitHub Pages keeps the public site free and easy to update.
  • The community software can be added later without redesigning the front door.
  • Discourse, NodeBB, or Flarum can live behind the same brand when you are ready.

Suggested phases

Phase 1

Launch this site, collect invite requests, and track the questions people submit.

Phase 2

Invite 25–100 founding members into a private beta and test the rooms/categories.

Phase 3

Install self-hosted Discourse or similar on a VPS when the community needs real accounts and threaded discussion.
