Platform Plan

Owned audience first. Fancy features later.

GitHub Pages is the polished front door. The live community can run later on an owned subdomain using Discourse, NodeBB, Flarum, or a custom backend when the community proves demand.

Recommended stack

Front door

GitHub Pages for this website: fast, free, professional, and easy to update.

Email capture

Use Wix, Google Forms, Tally, Brevo, or Formspree until a backend exists.

Community engine

Self-hosted Discourse is the strongest long-term forum/chat option when ready.

Domain

Use community.throupletea.com or community.3dudes1life.com for the actual member space.

Launch phases

Phase 1 — Waitlist

Publish this site, collect invite requests, and track what people are asking for.

Phase 2 — Founding circle

Invite a small group first. Keep moderation tight. Let the culture form before scaling.

Phase 3 — Owned community

Launch Discourse or similar on a subdomain with categories, profiles, DMs, and chat.

Phase 4 — Custom platform

Only build custom software after people are actively using the community and requesting specific features.
