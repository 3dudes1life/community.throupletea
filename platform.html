Why this order

No wasted money.

You get a premium-looking invite experience first, collect interest quietly, and avoid paying platform fees before there is enough activity to justify them.

When the first 50 are ready, the community backend becomes the private room behind this front door.

4. Prepare beta categories

Start with Introductions, Relationship Talk, Family, Living Together, Travel, Finances, Pets, and Podcast Talk.
