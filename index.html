People are asking where to talk.

Not where to date. Not where to find a third. Where to meet people who understand the questions, fear, family stuff, age gaps, living together, finances, jealousy, joy, and weird little real-life details.

Who this is for

Real relationships. Real support.

● Long-term throuples building a life together.
● New throuples navigating the first months or years.
● Triads seeking friendship, wisdom, and shared experience.
● People exploring the possibility with care and respect.
Who this is not for

Clear boundaries from day one.

● Hookup seekers.
● Unicorn hunters.
● Explicit content or adult solicitation.
● Dating-first behavior.
VIP rollout

Open in phases. Keep it special.

Phase 1

Founding 50

Invite-only private beta. Build the first posts, test categories, shape rules, and decide what needs to exist before launch.

Phase 2

Early Access 250

Founding Members can nominate trusted people. Still curated. Still moderated. Still no public free-for-all.

Phase 3

Public Waitlist

Once the culture is working, open broader applications while keeping approval controls and community guidelines firm.

Founding member promise

Help build the room you wish existed.

A place where someone can say, “I have so many questions I don’t even know what to ask first,” and be met with understanding instead of judgment.
