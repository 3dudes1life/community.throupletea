The Mission

No one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

The Throuple Tea Community is a welcoming, protective space where people can talk honestly about what it looks like to build a three-person relationship in the real world.

We are building culture before software: clear boundaries, thoughtful moderation, and a space where people can feel seen without being treated like a fantasy.
