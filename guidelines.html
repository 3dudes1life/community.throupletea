Community Guidelines

Friendship First. Support Always.

The Throuple Tea Community exists to help throuples, triads, and people exploring non-traditional relationships find connection, support, and real conversation.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships. We believe no one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

Who This Community Is For

  • Established Throuples & Triads: People actively living in a three-person relationship.
  • New Throuples: Relationships in their first days, months, or years.
  • People Exploring the Possibility: Individuals or couples curious about throuple relationships.
  • Real-Life Relationship Builders: People interested in communication, jealousy, family acceptance, living together, finances, travel, parenting, and long-term success.
  • Allies & Supporters: Close friends, family members, therapists, and professionals who genuinely want to better understand throuple relationships.

Who This Community Is NOT For

  • Hookup Seekers: This is not a place to find casual sexual encounters.
  • Unicorn Hunting: This is not a marketplace for couples seeking a third partner.
  • Dating-First Behavior: Friendship and community come first.
  • Explicit Sexual Content: Keep discussions respectful and appropriate for a diverse membership.
  • Fetishization: Members are people, not fantasies.
  • Harassment or Discrimination: No hate speech, bullying, personal attacks, or discrimination of any kind.

Core Values

Authenticity

Share real experiences, not performative perfection.

Respect

Different relationships may look different. Lead with curiosity.

Privacy

What is shared in the community stays in the community.

Support

We lift each other up. Advice should be kind, grounded, and non-shaming.

Growth

Nobody has all the answers. We are all learning.

Friendship First

The goal is meaningful connection, not transactions.

Moderation Promise

We reserve the right to remove posts, comments, or members that violate the spirit of this community. Safety, respect, and trust matter more than growth.
