Community Guidelines

Friendship First. Support Always.

These guidelines define the culture before the platform grows. The goal is safety, connection, and real conversation — not chaos, hookups, or performative drama.

Our Mission

To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships. We believe no one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

Who this community is for

Long-term throuples

Established relationships looking for friendship, support, and shared wisdom.

New throuples

Relationships in their first days, months, or years looking for grounded support.

Triads

Three-person relationships that want respectful, real-life conversations.

People exploring the possibility

Individuals or couples learning respectfully before making big decisions.

Who this community is not for

🚫

Hookup seekers
This is not a place to find casual encounters.

🚫

Unicorn hunters
This is not a marketplace for couples seeking a third partner.

🚫

Dating-first behavior
Friendship, support, and community come first.

🚫

Explicit sexual content
Keep discussions respectful and appropriate for a diverse membership.

🚫

Fetishization
Members are people, not fantasies.

🚫

Harassment or discrimination
No hate speech, bullying, personal attacks, outing, or discrimination of any kind.

Core values

Authenticity

Share real experiences, not performative perfection.

Respect

Different relationships look different. Lead with curiosity.

Privacy

What is shared in the community stays in the community.

Support

Advice should be kind, grounded, and non-shaming.

Growth

Nobody has all the answers. We are all learning.

Friendship First

The goal is meaningful connection, not transactions.

Moderation promise

Safety, respect, and trust matter more than growth. Posts, comments, or members that violate the spirit of this community may be removed. Boundary-pushers, spam, solicitation, explicit content, harassment, and dating-first behavior will be handled quickly.
