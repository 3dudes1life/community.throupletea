Community Guidelines

Culture first. Always.

These guidelines define the difference between a safe friendship-first community and another messy corner of the internet.

Who this is for

  • Long-term throuples
  • New throuples
  • Triads
  • People exploring the possibility
  • Allies and supporters

Who this is not for

  • Hookup seekers
  • Unicorn hunters
  • Explicit content
  • Dating-first behavior
  • Spam, promo, or fetishization

Core rules.

Simple, clear, enforceable.

🤝

Friendship first

No solicitation, “looking for a third,” hookup requests, or dating-first posts.

🧠

Assume humanity

Members are people, not fantasies, experiments, or content.

🔐

Respect privacy

Do not screenshot, repost, expose, or share private member stories outside the community.

🌈

No hate

No racism, homophobia, transphobia, misogyny, body shaming, or personal attacks.

🧹

No spam

No OnlyFans drops, unrelated business promos, scams, or repeated self-promotion.

🛟

Support, not therapy

Share lived experience and resources, but do not replace professional care or legal advice.
