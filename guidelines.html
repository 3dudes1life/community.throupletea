Culture Code

Friendship first. Support always.

These guidelines define the community before the platform grows. The goal is safety, connection, and real conversation — not chaos, hookups, or performative drama.

Mission

Why we exist

To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships.

We believe no one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

Positioning

What we are not

Not a dating app. Not a hookup site. Not a place to shop for a third.

This is a community for people trying to build healthy, respectful, real-life connection.

Membership Fit

Who belongs here

Long-term throuples

Established relationships looking for friendship, support, and shared wisdom.

New throuples

Relationships in their first days, months, or years looking for grounded support.

Triads

Three-person relationships that want respectful, real-life conversations.

Exploring members

People considering the possibility who want to learn with care before making major choices.

Allies

Friends, family, therapists, and supporters who want to better understand.

Relationship builders

People interested in communication, family, finances, living together, travel, parenting, and long-term success.

Boundaries

What gets removed

Hookup posts

No casual sexual solicitation or “who wants to play?” posts.

Unicorn hunting

No marketplace-style posts from couples seeking a third partner.

Explicit content

No adult content, explicit photos, sexual promotion, or OnlyFans-style marketing.

Dating-first behavior

Flirting, pressure, and DM chasing will be treated as safety issues.

Fetishization

Members are people, not fantasies or content.

Harassment

No hate speech, bullying, discrimination, outing, threats, or personal attacks.

Moderation Standard

Protect the room.

Moderators may remove posts, pause accounts, or close conversations that damage trust. Access is a privilege, especially during the Founding 50 phase.
