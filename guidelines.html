Culture Code

Friendship first. Support always.

These guidelines define the community before the platform grows. The goal is safety, connection, and real conversation — not chaos, hookups, or performative drama.

Mission

Why we exist

To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships.

We believe no one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

Membership Fit

Who this community is for

Long-term throuples

Established relationships looking for friendship, support, and shared wisdom.

New throuples

Relationships in their first days, months, or years looking for grounded support.

Triads

Three-person relationships that want respectful, real-life conversations.

People exploring

People considering the possibility who want to learn with care before making major choices.

Core Rules

Protect the room

  1. No hookup posts, sexual solicitation, or “who wants to play?” content.
  2. No unicorn-hunting marketplace posts from couples shopping for a third.
  3. No explicit photos, adult promotion, OnlyFans-style marketing, or fetish content.
  4. No pressure, DM chasing, harassment, discrimination, outing, or personal attacks.
  5. Respect privacy. Do not screenshot, repost, or share someone’s story without permission.
  6. Lead with support. Advice is welcome; shaming is not.

Moderation Standard

Access is a privilege

Moderators may remove posts, pause accounts, close conversations, or revoke access when behavior damages trust. During the Founding 50 phase, the standard is intentionally high because the first members shape the whole community.
