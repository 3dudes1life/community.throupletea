# Throuple Tea Community Blueprint

## Positioning

A friendship-first community for throuples, triads, and people exploring non-traditional relationships.

Not a dating app. Not a hookup site. Not a place to find a third.

## Mission

To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships.

We believe no one should have to navigate throuple life alone.

## Who this is for

- Long-term throuples
- New throuples
- Triads
- People exploring the possibility
- Allies, family, therapists, and professionals who want to understand respectfully

## Who this is not for

- Hookup seekers
- Unicorn hunters
- Explicit sexual content
- Dating-first behavior
- Fetishization
- Harassment, hate speech, or discrimination

## Founding member onboarding questions

1. What best describes you?
2. What are you hoping to find in this community?
3. Do you understand this is not a dating or hookup space?
4. Do you agree to respect privacy and avoid screenshots/sharing member stories outside the community?
5. What topics would help you feel less alone?

## Launch plan

### Phase 1: Landing Page + Waitlist
Collect interest, emails, and member needs. Track repeated questions.

### Phase 2: Founding Member Beta
Invite 25–100 people manually. Watch what conversations naturally happen.

### Phase 3: Private Community Launch
Launch on self-hosted Discourse, NodeBB, or Flarum. Keep it invite-based at first.

### Phase 4: Public Launch
Open applications to the wider audience through podcast, Instagram, website, and book readers.

## Moderation rules

- Remove dating solicitations quickly.
- Remove hookup posts immediately.
- No explicit images or sexual content.
- No screenshots from inside the community without consent.
- No outing people.
- No shaming relationship structures.
- No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination.
- Repeat boundary-pushers are removed.

## Brand voice

Warm, direct, protective, funny when appropriate, and deeply human.

The vibe is: “Come sit with us. You are not weird. You are not alone. But don’t bring trash energy into the room.”
