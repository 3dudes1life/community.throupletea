# Community Blueprint

## Mission
To create a welcoming, supportive community where throuples, triads, and those exploring non-traditional relationships can connect, learn, share experiences, and build meaningful friendships.

## Always Free Promise
The Throuple Tea Community will always offer free access. VIP means early, intentional, and culture-shaping — not paid.

## Who This Is For
- Long-term throuples
- New throuples
- Triads
- People exploring the possibility
- Allies and supporters

## Who This Is Not For
- Hookup seekers
- Unicorn hunters
- Explicit content
- Dating-first behavior
- Fetishization
- Spam or unrelated promotion

## Launch Phases
1. Founding Members: first 50 accepted applicants.
2. Early Access: up to 250 invite-first members.
3. Community Beta: up to 1,000 members.
4. Public Applications: open when safety and moderation are ready.

## Culture Principle
The first 50 members define the next 500. The next 500 define the next 5,000.
