# VIP Launch Plan

## Positioning

The Throuple Tea Community should launch as a Founding Members Program, not a public open forum.

## Phase 1 — First 50 Founding Members

Purpose: test the culture, categories, rules, onboarding, and moderation.

Access: application-only.

Member benefits:
- Founding Member badge
- Early access before public launch
- Ability to shape features
- Invite input for future phases
- First access to virtual events

## Phase 2 — Early Access: 250 Members

Invite-only expansion. Founding Members can nominate aligned people.

## Phase 3 — Community Launch: 1,000 Members

Public applications open, but the community remains moderated and intentional.

## Phase 4 — Open Platform

Wider public access, regional groups, events, resources, and possible sponsor/revenue options.

## Do not compromise

- No hookups
- No unicorn hunting
- No dating-first behavior
- No explicit content
- No fetishizing members
