FAQ

Clear answers before people apply.

The more specific the positioning is, the safer the community will feel.

Is this free?

Yes. The Throuple Tea Community will always offer free access. Founding Member access is early and intentional, not paid.

Is this a dating app?

No. This is not a dating app, hookup space, or unicorn-hunting marketplace. It is focused on friendship, support, learning, and real conversations.

Who can apply?

Long-term throuples, new throuples, triads, people exploring the possibility, allies, supporters, and thoughtful professionals may apply.

Why phased access?

Because the first members set the culture. We would rather grow carefully than open the doors and lose the safety that makes the space valuable.

Will there be private messaging?

The future platform may include direct messages, but moderation and safety will guide how features roll out.

Will my information be sold?

No. This exists because people need a safer place outside large platforms, not because we want to sell member data.
