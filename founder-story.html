Why We Built This

Because people kept asking where they belong.

This community started from a simple, repeated question: where can throuples and triads talk to people who actually understand this life — outside dating apps, hookup groups, and giant platforms?

Not investors. Not a dating company. Us.

We have lived the questions people are asking — family, commitment, misunderstanding, visibility, daily life, and finding a way to build something real as three people in a world built for two. The podcast, the book, and the stories brought people to us. This community lets those people find each other.

We are building it off the large platforms because community should not disappear because of an algorithm, a policy change, or a company that never understood us in the first place.

The article can introduce people to us. The community gives them somewhere to go next.

That is the handoff: read the story, feel seen, then apply to help build a safer place for throuples and triads to connect.

What we know

People are isolated, curious, scared, excited, and looking for real examples.

What we refuse

Turning throuple life into fetish content, hookup ads, or a dating marketplace.

What we are building

A safe, free, friendship-first place for people navigating real relationships.
