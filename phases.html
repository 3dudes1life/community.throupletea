VIP Launch Plan

Small first. Strong first.

The community opens in curated phases so the culture can grow without turning into another messy internet group.

Phase 1

Founding 50

Invite-only private beta for the first 50 people. This group tests the structure, starts the first conversations, and helps shape the guidelines.

  • Founding Member badge
  • Private access before public launch
  • Input on features and categories
  • 30–60 day culture-building period
Phase 2

Early Access 250

Founding Members can nominate trusted people. This keeps the vibe curated while expanding conversation volume.

  • Invite-only growth
  • More topic rooms
  • First virtual meetups
  • Moderation stress test
Phase 3

Public Waitlist

Applications open wider, but access is still approved. The site can promote the waitlist without opening the doors to everyone at once.

  • Website waitlist
  • Relationship-status questions
  • Community needs survey
  • Slow approval batches
Phase 4

Public Launch

Only after the community culture is stable should the platform move toward broader public access.

  • Public landing page
  • Clear rules
  • Moderation team
  • Owned email list and platform backend
