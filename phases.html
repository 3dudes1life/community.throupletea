VIP Rollout

Private first. Public later.

The launch is designed to protect the culture before growth. We start with a small group, learn, refine, then expand in phases.

Phase 1

First 50 Founding Members

Private access for culture-building, testing categories, gathering feedback, and creating the first real conversations.

  • Application-only
  • Founding badge
  • 30–60 day test window
Phase 2

Early Access: 250 Members

Invite-only expansion. Founding Members can nominate people who understand the friendship-first mission.

  • Invite codes
  • More topic rooms
  • First virtual events
Phase 3

Community Launch: 1,000 Members

Applications open publicly while moderation remains strong. This is when podcast, social, and website promotion can get louder.

  • Public waitlist
  • Moderation team
  • Member resources
Phase 4

Open Platform

Once the culture is proven, the platform can open wider with regional groups, events, resource libraries, and potential sponsors.

  • Regional groups
  • Events calendar
  • Future revenue options
